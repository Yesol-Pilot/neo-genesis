NIST AI RMF checklist

The NIST AI Risk Management Framework is voluntary guidance for managing AI risks. This checklist turns its lifecycle idea into internal evidence questions.

Govern

Document policy, accountability, roles, risk tolerance, and escalation paths.

Map and measure

Define context, stakeholders, harms, evaluation methods, and measurement limits.

Manage

Prioritize mitigations, monitor residual risk, and record accepted tradeoffs.

Reference official NIST materials when using this page for formal governance work.
