EU AI Act readiness checklist

This non-legal readiness page helps teams organize AI system inventory, risk classification, documentation, and control evidence before specialist review.

Inventory

List each AI system, provider or deployer role, intended use, users, geography, and affected stakeholders.

Risk review

Separate prohibited, high-risk, transparency, general-purpose, and low-risk questions for legal validation.

Evidence

Prepare technical documentation, human oversight, monitoring, incident handling, and quality management records.

  • Use official EU materials and counsel for binding interpretation.
  • Keep change logs when system use, model version, or market changes.
  • Do not treat this checklist as legal advice.
