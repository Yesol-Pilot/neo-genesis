Retention and churn causal analysis

Retention programs often target users who already look different. Causal analysis keeps lifecycle decisions from confusing risk scores with treatment effects.

Treatment definition

Document who received the lifecycle message, discount, concierge support, or product prompt.

Outcome window

Define survival, repeat activity, paid renewal, or downgrade prevention over the right horizon.

Bias controls

Account for pre-treatment activity, tenure, plan, support history, and acquisition channel.

Useful reads

  • Compare treatment effects by lifecycle stage.
  • Track delayed churn after short-term incentive campaigns.
  • Report both saved users and cost per saved user.
